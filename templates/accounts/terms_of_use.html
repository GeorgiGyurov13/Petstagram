Community Guidelines

Respect Others

Treat everyone—including pets—with kindness and respect.

  • Be polite in your interactions with others.
  • Avoid offensive language and behavior.
  • Listen to and consider different perspectives.

Content Guidelines

Ensure your content is appropriate and follows our guidelines.

  • Do not post inappropriate or explicit content.
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights.
  • Report any content that violates our guidelines.

Pet Posting Guidelines

Make sure your pet posts adhere to our community standards.

  • Only post photos and videos of your own pets.
  • Avoid sharing content that promotes harm or cruelty towards animals.
  • Respect other users' pets and avoid making negative comments.
